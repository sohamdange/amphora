Amphora

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 5, 2026

Short version: Amphora collects absolutely nothing about you. Your ideas stay on your device. No servers, no accounts, no tracking — ever.

Data Collection

Amphora does not collect any personal data, usage data, or analytics of any kind. There are no crash reporters, no event trackers, no telemetry pipelines, and no third-party SDKs that observe your behavior.

We do not know who you are, how often you use the app, which features you use, or what ideas you store. That information exists only on your device and is inaccessible to us.

Data Storage

All data — your ideas, settings, and API keys — is stored exclusively in a local SQLite database on your device. Nothing is written to external servers or cloud storage.

  • Ideas and their content are stored on-device only
  • App settings (theme, AI provider preferences) are stored on-device only
  • API keys, if entered, are stored encrypted on-device only
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data
Amphora has no backend, no database server, and no cloud infrastructure. There is no Amphora server that your data ever touches.

AI Features

AI features in Amphora are entirely optional. The app is fully functional without them. If you choose to enable AI, you supply your own API key from a supported provider (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google Gemini, or Groq).

When you use an AI feature, the relevant idea content and your API key are sent directly from your device to that AI provider's servers. This request does not pass through any Amphora server or intermediary — it is a direct connection between your device and the provider you chose.

Your use of each AI provider is governed by that provider's own privacy policy and terms of service. Amphora has no control over, and no visibility into, what those providers do with the data you send them.

  • AI is opt-in — the app works fully without it
  • You provide and own your own API key
  • API keys are stored encrypted on your device and never logged
  • Idea content is sent only to the provider you select, never to Amphora
  • You can remove your API key at any time in Settings

Third Parties

Amphora does not share any data with third parties. The only outbound network requests the app can make are direct calls to an AI provider's API — and only when you have explicitly enabled AI features and triggered an AI action.

There are no advertising networks, no analytics services, no social SDKs, and no data brokers involved in this app.

Children's Privacy

Amphora does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Because no data is collected at all, the app poses no special risk to younger users beyond the general consideration that AI providers have their own age requirements for API usage.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL with a revised effective date. Because no data is collected, any changes would only reflect clarifications or updates to how optional AI features work.
